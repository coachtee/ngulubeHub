## The model

Each contact gets exactly **3 touch points** in August, spaced 7-10 days apart:

1. **Day 0** — Personalised intro (use the **Generate Intro** button → print/PDF → send)
2. **Day 7-10** — A "value drop" message: a 2-minute Loom or a one-page insight tied to their sector (no pitch)
3. **Day 17-20** — The "30-min ask" with a calendar link

Don't send a 4th touch. If they don't reply after 3, move them to a nurture list and revisit in November/January.

## The intro formula (built into Ngulube Hub)

The system already produces a one-page brief per contact. To make it land, customise these three things before you press send:

1. **First line** — the `Hello Teddy,` opener is fine, but replace the sector hook with a sentence that shows you read their actual work. Example: "Saw your post about X — that's exactly the problem we solve for ____."
2. **The "what I noticed" section** — keep 2-3 of their pain points, edit them so they sound like *you* saw them, not a template.
3. **The 30-min next step** — send with a real Calendly/Cal.com link, not a placeholder.

## Tracking (Ngulube Hub does this for you)

- Open the person's profile
- Click **Mark Intro Sent** (auto-logs and changes status to "Intro sent")
- After each call/WhatsApp, log it in the **Activity Log** on the right of the profile
- Move statuses as conversations progress: `Intro sent` → `Meeting scheduled` → `Engaged` → `Won`/`Lost`
